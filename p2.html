Biblically Accurate Heaven

For this 3D model I made made a landscape of my version of what Heaven could look like. I am not religious, but I decide to make this after I was watching video about how Ezekiel saw angels , and how heaven looked. So, I added my own mix of what heaven could look like. I added like simple angels, but I added two biblically accurate suns. I made it shiny to give it the effect that heaven is this really holy place that is shiny even at night. I used blender to make the whole thing. Click the picture and link to see another angle of the 3D model. Want to see a different Angle click me

Cheerleaders Guide: To survivng a serial killer

This project is a trialer for a series of mine called, "Cheerleaders Guide: To surviving a serial killer". I made this trailer when I just started making the series, and I wanted to try making 3D animation. The process was a little hard since I was new to it, but I overall enjoyed making the trailer. The person in the video is a character of mind. Her name is Koko who is a cheerleader, that is taking on a role of dective to find the killer in her college causing the dissaperences in the cheer team. I used blender for the 3D modeling, and Capcut to edit the video to add dialogue and sound.
