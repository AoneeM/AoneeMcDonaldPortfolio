When you’re part of the country’s best cheer team you would think being able to compete to become the next Head Cheer captain with some others would be fun, and you’d slay in this competition. WELL someone took slay LITERALLY and started to literally SLAY the cheerleaders. To makes matters worse this Psycho Cheer Killer is dress as the school mascot Mr. Smiley so it could be literally anyone So much for cheer spirit Lets GO! UCP Smileys! My ass, the cops are taking their sweet ass time interviewing the wrong people, and Mr. Smiley is going around killing off Cheerleaders! Well we don’t know about you, but we’re not going to sit around to find out if we’re next on Mr. Smiley's hit list. There is a pattern as to why this person targets specifically cheerleaders. Crazy idea but what if we do our own investigation? This detective shit gotta be easy, and since we’re also part of the cheer squad we’d be closer to finding out the truth There’s gotta be some kind of way to do this though without Mr. Smiley possibly showing up to our door next. Like some sort of guide Join koko and yume two cheerleaders on a mission to caught this random masked person, all while trying to keep find out the cheer teams darkest secrets.
